Nivetha Pethuraj: U24 Badminton Gold Medalist (2024)


Nivetha Pethuraj is a rising star in the world of badminton, having recently won the 2024 Badminton U24 Championship Gold Medal. Her exceptional performance and relentless determination on the court earned her the top spot, showcasing her skill, agility, and strategic gameplay. Nivetha’s victory at this prestigious event marks a major milestone in her badminton career, solidifying her position as one of the sport's promising young talents. Her dedication and hard work continue to inspire budding athletes across the globe.
